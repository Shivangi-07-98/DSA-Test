int[] arr = {10, 20, 30}
for(int num : arr) → num catches 10, then 20, then 30
num is int, not int[]

ArrayList<String> str = ["apple", "banana"]
for(String fruit : str) → fruit catches "apple", then "banana"
fruit is String, not ArrayList<String>

The variable type in for-each loop must match the element type, not the collection type!
